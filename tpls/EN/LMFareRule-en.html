General FIT Fare Rule

Rule No. GR01

Content ATPCO Category Economy
Booking Class - Y/B/H K/L/M N/S/X Q/T/V O/E
Unaccompanied Minors 01 Not applicable
Flight Application 04 LQ operating flights only.
Reservations/Ticketing 05 Confirmed reservation must be made for entire journey. Normal system ticketing deadline applied.
Minimum Stay 06 For international routings into-REP: 1D; for others: no restriction.
Maximum Stay 07 1Y 6M 1M 14D
Stopovers 08 Permitted without charges.
Class Mixing and Open Jaw 10 A) Permitted for public fares or for private fares listed under the same tour code. B) Fare is calculated as sum of 50% round-trip prices respectively of both booking classes. Not applicable.
Endorse/Reroute 16 Not permitted.
No-show fee 16 USD 10 per ticket. Not applicable.
Rebook 16 Rebooking fee USD 20 per ticket per transaction. Rebooking fee USD 30 per ticket per transaction. Not permitted.
A) Rebooked to higher class(fare): fare difference would be charged.
B) Rebooked to lower class(fare): residual fare value is non-refundable.
* Rebooking is permitted for public fares or for private fares listed under the same tour code.
* Passengers need to cancel the original flight(s) 2 hours before departure; otherwise, no-show fee will be charged.
Refund 16 USD 30 per ticket. USD 40 per ticket. Non-refundable.
* Passengers need to cancel the original flight(s) 1 day before departure; otherwise, no-show fee will be charged.
* Refund is only permitted for totally unused ticket. For partially used ticket: non-refundable.
Ticket Endorsement Box 18 NON-RER/END, REB USD20, REF USD30, NOSH USD10 NON-RER/END, REB USD30, REF USD40, NOSH USD10 NON-RER/END/REB/REF
Child (between 2 and 12 years old) Discount 19 Charge 75% of corresponding adult fare. Please insert "/CH25" in fare basis.
Infant (under 2 years old) Discount 19 A)Charge 10% of corresponding adult fare (without seat offered). Please insert "/IN90" in fare basis.
B)Charge 75% of corresponding adult fare (with seat offered). Please insert "/IN25" in fare basis.

Remarks:

1. All fares are exclusive of taxes and other surcharges.

2. Please refer to GDS display for latest update on flight schedule and fares.

3. No child discount is applied for Rebooking/No-show/Refund/Handling Fee and other charges.

4. Departure date of the first outbound sector determines the fare for the entire journey.

5. All rebooking must be applied within ticket validity.

6. For any discrepancy between this General Fare Rule and GDS, details in GDS prevail.
