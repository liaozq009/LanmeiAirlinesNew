Remarks:

1. All fares are exclusive of taxes and other surcharges.

2. Please refer to GDS display for latest update on flight schedule and fares.

3. No child discount is applied for Rebooking/No-show/Refund/Handling Fee and other charges.

4. Infants are exempt from Rebooking Fee and Refund Fee.

5. Departure date of the first outbound sector determines the fare for the entire journey.

6. All rebooking must be applied within ticket validity.

7. For any discrepancy between this General Fare Rule and GDS, details in GDS prevail.
