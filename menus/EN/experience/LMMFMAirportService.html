Macau International Airport guide

Ready to fly?

Get your trip off to a flying start with Macau International Airport. Don't worry. From check-in details to airport facilities for departures and subsequent travel information for arrivals, we've got everything covered.

  • Which terminal?

    There is only one Terminal in Macau International Airport, so it’s easy for you to find the ticket counter and check-in counter.

  • When does my flight depart/arrive?

    Unsure of your time arrangement? It's best to be safe than sorry. Check your departure and arrival time with our handy flight status tool.

    Flight status

  • What time should I arrive at the airport?

    Leave those last-minute airport dashes to the big screen! We recommend that you arrive at least two hours before your flight is due to leave.

  • Do I need a visa?

    Check out our Visa information pages for every country that we fly to.

    Visa information

  • Online check in

    The best way to check in is online where you can pick your seat and print your boarding card. If you’re travelling with carry-on baggage only, you can print off your own boarding pass and we’ll see you at the gate. At present, check-in online is not supported in Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, Sihanoukville, Macau and Palau.

    Check in online now

  • Counter check in and bag drop

    It’s easy for you to find the check-in counter. Check-in service starts from four hours before departure and closes one hour before departure.

    Baggage allowance
