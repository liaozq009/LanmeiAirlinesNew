Easy check-in at a self-service kiosk

Online check-in is the fastest and simplest way of check-in. If you are unable to check in online, you can still check in at one of our self-service kiosks at the airport.

In just a couple of easy steps, you can check in by yourself and for your travelling companions. When you check in online, you can choose your favourite seat on board.

This is how the kiosk works:

Step 1

Insert your passport or frequent flyer card, or enter your ticket number or booking code. Your booking details will appear on the screen.

Step 2

Select your seat on the seating map.

Step 3

Print your boarding pass.

Step 4

Do you have checked baggage? Simply hand it over at the nearest baggage drop-off point, and proceed to the gate. Have a good trip!

If you have already checked in, you can also use the kiosks to print your boarding pass. At most other airports, the kiosks are available for checking in and printing boarding passes at least two hours before flight departure.
