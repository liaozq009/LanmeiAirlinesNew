Rates of Baggage and Seat Selection

1. Carry-on Baggage

For all routes operated by Lanmei Airlines, each passenger is allowed to carry ONE piece of carry-on baggage, which must not exceed 115cm (55cm x 40cm x 20cm) in size and 7kg in weight.

In addition to the baggage mentioned above, a passenger is entitled to bring 1 more small item, which must not exceed 80cm (40cm x 30cm x 10cm) in size, aboard the aircraft. These items include:

• Small handbag

• Laptop bag

• Small backpack

• Camera bag

• Briefcase

Any item exceeding the free carry-on baggage allowance must be checked in.

2. Checked Baggage

For international routes CAN=PNH, HKG=PNH and BKK=PNH, and Cambodian domestic route PNH=REP, each passenger occupying seats is entitled to 25kg free allowance of checked baggage.

For other routes, each passenger occupying seat is entitled to 15kg free allowance of checked baggage.

For the excess amount, charging rates are as follows.

2.1 Domestic Sectors within Cambodia

• USD 2 per kilogram, for pre-purchasing, 12h before departure.

• USD 3 per kilogram, at airport check-in counter.

Terms and Conditions

• The maximum weight and dimensions of any single piece of checked baggage should not be over 32kg and 158cm (Length + Width + Height) respectively. Overweight and oversize items (exception: mobility aids, sporting equipment, etc.) must be repacked or split into lighter units; otherwise will not be accepted for carriage.

• Baggage exceeding free allowance will only be accepted subject to the available space on board on the day of departure.

• The number of pieces of checked baggage is unlimited.

• Mobility aids, e.g. wheelchairs, baby carriages, etc. necessarily carried by a passenger can be accepted without charges as additional baggage allowance.

• Infants without occupying seats are not granted any complimentary carry-on or checked baggage allowance.

• Baggage allowance could be pooled only when passengers are in the same PNR.

• For what is left unmentioned about baggage policy in this memo, please refer to Lanmei Airlines General Conditions of Carriage of Passengers and Baggage.
