e-Visa information for Cambodia

The e-Visa

Officially launched in April, 2006, e-Visa Cambodia was created by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation, Kingdom of Cambodia and enables Citizens from eligible countries who hold a passport valid for at least six (6) months, to apply for a visa online. Applicants must apply online at least four (4) days before traveling to the Kingdom of Cambodia. An e-Visa Cambodia once issued is valid for three (3) months from the date of issue.

Don't worry about it! You can access e-Visa services website ( https://www.evisa.gov.kh/) to know whether you need to apply for a visa (if needed, you can apply for visa through this link online). If you fail to pay for the first time, please enter the URL (https://www.evisa.gov.kh/CheckChange.aspx) for payment again, and application number and preferred email are needed. It takes about 3 working days sending the Visa in PDF format to your preferred email. Please carry two copies of Visa typing in black and white A4 paper with you.
