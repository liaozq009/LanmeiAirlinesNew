Membership rating rules

1. Standards for Members

The rating cycle for upgrade is 1-7 working days after the date of each flight. If there is any question, please feel free to contact Lanmei Club (lanmeiclub@lanmeiairlines.com).

Rating Standards for Members

Member Level Rating Criteria Validity
Ordinary Member Registration Long-term and effective
Silver Member 20 qualifying segments Effective within 12 consecutive natural months
Gold Member 40 qualifying segments Effective within 12 consecutive natural months
Platinum Member 60 qualifying segments Effective within 12 consecutive natural months

For example:

If member A is upgraded to a silver member on March 12th, 2019, the number of qualifying segments shall be cleared; As of the 12th consecutive natural month, that is, March 11th, 2020, if member A has accumulated 40 qualifying segments, member A shall be upgraded to a gold member, and the number of qualifying segments shall be cleared.

Grade Retaining Standards for Members

The rating cycle for grade retaining is 12 consecutive natural months from the current membership level.

Member Level Grade Retaining Criteria
Ordinary Member Unlimited
Silver Member Accumulate 18 qualifying segments within 12 consecutive natural months
Gold Member Accumulate 32 qualifying segments within 12 consecutive natural months
Platinum Member Accumulate 42 qualifying segments within 12 consecutive natural months

①For example (grade retaining):

If member A is upgraded to a gold member on March 12th, 2019, the number of qualifying segments shall be cleared; As of the 12th consecutive natural month, that is, March 11th, 2020, if member A has accumulated 32 to 41 qualifying segments, member A shall still be a gold member, and the number of qualifying segments shall be cleared.

②For example (degradation):

If member A is upgraded to a gold member on March 12th, 2019, the number of qualifying segments shall be cleared; As of the 12th consecutive natural month, that is, March 11th, 2020, if member A has merely accumulated 1 to 31 qualifying segments or fail to accumulate 32 qualifying segments, member A shall be degraded to a silver member, and the number of qualifying segments shall be cleared.

* If the member fails to reach the grade retaining standard within 12 consecutive natural months, the membership shall be lowered by one level.

2. Accumulation of Lanmei Coins and Qualifying Segments

Members are entitled to accumulate Lanmei coins and qualifying segments after improve personal information; if you need to modify your personal information, please feel free to contact Lanmei Club.

After a member in person taking flight with Lanmei Airlines, the accumulative Lanmei coins for the member shall be equal to the net fare of the member’s ticket multiplied by the basic accumulative proportion of Lanmei coins. If the passenger is not the member in person, Lanmei coins and qualifying segments shall not be credited to the account of this member.

If ordinary, silver and gold members purchase tickets of class Q, T, V, P, O, E , U, G and A, Lanmei coins shall not be accumulated; If platinum members purchase tickets of class Q, T, V, P, O, E and U class, the basic accumulative proportion of Lanmei coins and qualifying segments shall be in accordance with that of silver members.

If members succeed in participating designated activities of Lanmei Airlines, the accumulation of Lanmei coins and qualifying segments shall be in accordance to the specific activity rules. The accumulative number of Lanmei coins and qualifying segments shall be credited to the member center within 1-7 working days at the end of activities.

Basic Accumulative Proportion of Lanmei Coins
Ordinary member: ticket net price*100%
Silver member: ticket net price* 120%
Gold member: ticket net price* 140%
Platinum member: ticket net price* 160%（For grade retaining, no more upgrade）
Non-platinum member: Non-cumulative Class：Q、T、V、P、O、E、U、G、A
Members can accumulate qualifying segments by booking charter flights , but not Lanmei coins.
