旅行日记

金边

柬埔寨(金边皇宫)

金边皇宫也称四臂湾大王宫，因位于上湄公河、洞里萨河、下湄公河与巴萨河的交汇处而得名，是诺罗敦国王于1866-1870年建造的，是柬埔寨国王的皇宫。
王宫的建筑具有高棉传统建筑风格和宗教色彩，宫殿均有尖塔，代表繁荣；殿身涂以黄、白两色，黄色代表佛教，白色代表婆罗门教,大小宫殿二十多座。

Phnom Penh

Cambodia,Royal Palace

The Royal Palace of Phnom Penh, also known as the four-arm bay palace, was named because of the river junction among the Up-Mekong River, the Tonle Sap River, the Down- Mekong River and the Bassac River. It was built by King Norodom in 1866-1870 to be the palace of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The building of palaces has traditional Khmer architectural style with religious characters. All palaces have a minaret, which has the meaning of prosperity; the hall of palaces was painted by yellow and white, yellow represents Buddhism, white represents Brahmanism, the scale of palaces is more than twenty.
